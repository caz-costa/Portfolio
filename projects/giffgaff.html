D&AD: Giff Gaff

Reboot?

Tell the giffgaff recycle story with illustration and animation.

Final outcomes must include:

A 20 second TV spot (you can present this as of a series of stills, an animated video or a storyboard) showing the four easy steps to recycling your phone (and mentioning that it’s sustainable too!)

Instagram story assets.

Digital display banners.

With giffgaff's quirky nature and their history of exciting adverts, I wanted to make sure that mine felt part of that family. From research many people tend to keep their old unused phones due to either sentimentality or forgetfulness, therefore, I wanted to play with the feeling of nostalgia by throwing it back to retro style games.

The aim was to show that your old phone can be rebooted and given another adventure. The story of our phone is, it is picked as the hero of our story, however, it dies one too many times and our player gives up. But, when the ‘game over’ screen shows, it’s shattered by our giffgaff reboot feature. All the player has to do to restart our hero is to send it to giffgaff recycle and they will get money for their old phone. Our hero gets a second chance at an adventure with a new player.
