Double Decker

Take the supermarket’s most shocking packaging designs and turn them into something you’d be proud to put in your trolley. Craft bold & beautiful graphics. Inspire us with your structural vision. Get wordy with a name that’s going to change people’s minds about your produt. This is your opportunity to end the autopilot in our supermarket aisles.

The faithful egg. A staple in many households, but its packaging is sub-par, many in either beige packaging or the ugly plastic casings. Additionally, its bulky packaging only allows for one egg box in the fridge. Therefore, the focus of the design alteration centres around two core concepts; repackaging in a double-decked structure allowing increased number of eggs per horizontal length, and the intent to supply positive news through feel-good news stories incorporated within the packaging’s branding – a concept that is more likely to entice consumers towards the product as a way to escape from the usual bad news due COVID and other matters, in what has been a challenging period for so many.

The branding focuses on the different colours to attract the customers as well as bold and playful fonts to relate to a bigger target audience. Its playful tone of voice also provides an uplifting feel during unprecedented times. The touch points allow for that tone of voice to shine, providing humour in regard to the branding on a double decker bus. The poster shows a typical breakfast spread with the traditional ‘bad news’ newspaper covered by the double decker packaging and the ‘eggcellent news’. Now that faithful egg has turned into the hero of the supermarket, providing its customers with a source of laughter, right at the start of the day.
