Type: Conceptual Characters

Canela

Choose a typeface from the given lists, use the name of your typeface as the starting point for your project and develop a final outcome from it.

The chosen font was Canela which was Spanish and Portuguese for Cinnamon, designed by Miguel Reyes, this led me to look at publications covering the topic of cinnamon. The selected publication was a children’s book by Neil Gainman, called Cinnamon. With the focus of the book centred around a blind princess called Cinnamon, I wanted the publication to be friendly for blind and partially sighted children and adults. This led to the initial funky shaped book; however, this was unpractical as the images would be punched through to get the braille.

Nevertheless, this initial idea created the basis of type paths creating the images, which later became one long line of text in the shape of key elements of the book. This meant that the publication could no longer be a traditional book form, instead, a banner was more beneficial as it could fit both the continuous text path and the braille.
